At C-SED we offer exclusive learning opportunities and career options to social entrepreneur from the rural and semi urban background.

WHO WE ARE

C-SED stand for centre for social entrepreneurship development an unique initiative by ESTAH which is a bridge that connects social entrepreneurs with others who want to help them along the way, such as volunteers, mentors, and coaches, through a self-sustaining asset mapping strategy. Social entrepreneurs connect the two different words namely business and society by creating innovative solutions that are easily available easily accessible and affordable to the common people.

WHAT WE DO

As C-SED is working as bridge for social entrepreneur and people who want to assist them, so through the asset mapping technique, C-SED creates a favorable climate for prospective social entrepreneurs to turn their business ideas into reality. The allocation of all resources, such as online presence, is known as asset mapping.
